CLIMATE CHANGE EFFECT

How Does Climate Change Effect Whales?

Whales struggle to adapt to the changes in our climate. Ways that they can be affected are warmer oceans and melting ice caps can jeopardise the whales food sources. The Bowhead, Beluga and Narwhal are particularly in danger. Climate change can also affect the whales migration patterns. Also the depletion of the ozone layers and the rise in UV(ultraviolet)radiation can also kill krill, which is a whale's favorite food source.
