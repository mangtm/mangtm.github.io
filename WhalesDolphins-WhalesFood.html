What Do Whales Eat?

This depends on what type of whale, how big they are, their environment or if they are baeleen or tooth whales. Whales diets can change severely. Their food can range from small aquatic life forms like, shrimp, fish, crab, plankton, krill and squid to larger marine animals like sea lions, seals, sharks, walruses, seabirds and other whales.
