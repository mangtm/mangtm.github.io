Where do Dolphins Live?

Dolphins live all over the planet. In oceans and in some rivers.There is a species of dolphins for each environment. For example,bottlenose dolphins live in every ocean except the arctic and antartic oceans. Another species,the Atlantic spotted dolphin,lives in tropical and temperate areas in the Atlantic Ocean. They also don't live in Arctic and Antartic Oceans.
