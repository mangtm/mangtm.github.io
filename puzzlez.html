The Amazon River Dolphin

The Amazon river dolphin (Boto or Pink river dolphin) is found in the Amazon river and the joining rivers, and is the largest species of freshwater dolphin. The Amazon river dolphin feeds on crabs, fish and catfish and is rarely hunted by animals other than humans.

The Big Blue Whale

Blue whales are the largest animals ever known to have lived on Earth. These magnificent marine mammals rule the oceans at up to 100 feet long and upwards of 200 tons. Their tongues alone can weigh as much as an elephant. Their hearts, weigh as much as an automobile.
