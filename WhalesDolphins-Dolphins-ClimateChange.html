How does climate change effect dolphins?

Climate Change is a major threat for dolphins.The green house effect causes climate change. People cause the green house effect because things we do like driving a car, or any motor powered vehicle emmits carbon dioxide which traps heat. Also factories emmit carbon dioxide. Climate change effects dolphins by destroying their habitat, food and the rise in sea temperatures.
